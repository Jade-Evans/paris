Wander Through Paris

Quartiers and Gardens

  • 🖼️ Montmartre

    Where painters line the square and lamplight hits just right. Artistic and nostalgic, Montmartre still hums with the spirit of Toulouse-Lautrec and bohemian Paris.

  • 🌿 Promenade Plantée (Coulée Verte René-Dumont)

    An elevated garden path growing from a forgotten rail line — wildflowers overhead, secret courtyards below. Quiet magic in the 12th.

  • 🌸 Jardin des Plantes

    Botanical wonder meets 17th-century charm. Glass greenhouses, tree-lined paths, and a whiff of natural science. A garden made for musing.

  • 🎨 Rue Crémieux

    A single candy-colored street that feels lifted from a Wes Anderson set. Residents may sigh at its popularity, but you’ll understand why.

  • 🏛️ Latin Quarter

    Tangle through narrow medieval lanes where students have debated since the 13th century. Bookshops, ivy, and echoes of the Sorbonne.

  • 🌊 Along the Seine

    Follow the riverbanks — from Notre-Dame to Pont Alexandre III. Bouquinistes, bridges, sunsets. A walk that never disappoints.

  • ✨ Champs-Élysées to Arc de Triomphe

    Grand, glowing, unapologetically Parisian. Best savored late at night, when the tourists thin and the city feels cinematic.
