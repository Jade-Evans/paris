Bienvenue a Paris!

Discover all Paris has to offer with this selection of 3-day itineraries.

Classic Parisian Charm

Immerse yourself in the iconic art, history, and culture of Paris — from world-renowned museums to cozy cafés, discover the heart and soul of the city.

Flavours of Paris

Centered on the city's culinary magic, this journey includes a market stroll in Le Marais, wine and cheese in Saint-Germain, and a food tour near Notre-Dame — with cultural detours to the Eiffel Tower and sunset views from Sacré-Cœur.

Romantic Parisian Getaway

A dreamy itinerary for couples craving intimate moments — start your day with fresh pastries, explore the art-filled streets of Montmartre, cruise under the Eiffel Tower at dusk, and end with dinner at a charming bistro. Romance meets culture and cuisine in every step.
