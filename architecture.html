  • Eiffel Tower

    The iconic iron lattice tower soaring above Paris, offering panoramic views from its observation decks

  • Notre-Dame Cathedral

    A masterpiece of Gothic architecture, famed for its soaring spires, stained glass, and haunting gargoyles.

  • Sainte chapelle

    A hidden gem known for its breathtaking 13th-century stained glass windows that bathe the chapel in colorful light.

  • Arc de Triomphe

    A monumental arch honoring France’s military history, standing proudly at the top of the Champs-Élysées.

  • Palais Garnier

    An opulent opera house with a dazzling chandelier, gilded interiors, and a ceiling painted by Chagall.

  • Luxembourg Palace

    A grand former royal residence nestled in the serene Luxembourg Gardens, now home to the French Senate.

  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica

    A striking white-domed church perched atop Montmartre, offering sweeping views of Paris and a serene spiritual ambiance.
