Parisian Itinerary: Hidden Gems

Discover some of Paris' lesser known attractions with this two-day itinerary

🗓 Hidden Gems

🌞 Morning - Hidden Art & Culture

  • Le Musée Marmottan Monet – A hidden treasure for art lovers, featuring an incredible collection of Monet's works.
  • Musée de la Vie Romantique – A museum dedicated to the Romantic era, housed in a charming house tucked away in the 9th arrondissement.
  • Canal Saint-Martin – This picturesque canal offers a quiet, scenic walk away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

🍽 Lunch - Secret Gardens & Parks

  • Parc des Buttes-Chaumont – One of the most beautiful parks in Paris, complete with a lake, waterfall, and suspension bridge.
  • Jardin des Serres d’Auteuil – A series of stunning glasshouses and gardens in the 16th arrondissement, filled with exotic plants.
  • Jardin du Ranelagh – A peaceful park perfect for a leisurely stroll or picnic, with a picturesque pond and lovely flower beds.

🏛 Afternoon - Hidden Bars & Cafés

  • Le Comptoir Général – A quirky, eclectic bar with a hidden tropical garden in the back.
  • Le Syndicat – A speakeasy-style bar in the 10th arrondissement, specializing in unique cocktails made with French spirits.
  • Café de Flore – While not exactly hidden, it's a quintessential Parisian café often overshadowed by other landmarks; perfect for people-watching and enjoying a coffee.

🌇 Evening - Underrated Neighborhoods

  • Le Marais – Known for its historic charm, boutique shops, and trendy cafés, this neighborhood often gets overlooked by tourists but offers a fantastic blend of old and new.
  • La Villette – A vibrant cultural hub with modern architecture, parks, and the Cité des Sciences, perfect for those who want to experience a different side of Paris.
  • Oberkampf – A lively, artistic neighborhood known for its cool bars, street art, and unique vibe.
