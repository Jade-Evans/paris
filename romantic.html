Parisian Itinerary: Romantic

Spend quality time with the one you love in one of the most romantic cities

🗓 Day 1

☕️ Breakfast - Hardware Société near Sacré-Cœur

Begin your romantic morning with a creative Australian-style breakfast at Hardware Société, nestled just steps from the iconic Sacré-Cœur. Its cozy vibe and beautifully plated dishes make it a perfect start before exploring Montmartre. Morning visits are quieter, giving you time to savor both the food and the view.

  • Recommended Duration: 1 hour
  • Travel Time to next stop: 5 mins walk to Montmartre

🌞 Morning - Montmartre walk + visit Sacré-Cœur

Wander the cobbled streets of Montmartre, Paris’s most romantic hilltop neighborhood. Explore the Place du Tertre’s artists, then climb to Sacré-Cœur for sweeping views. The morning is quieter, ideal for soaking in the bohemian charm and hand-in-hand moments away from the crowds.

  • Recommended Duration: 2 hours
  • Travel Time to next stop: 5 mins walk to Le Consulat

🍽 Lunch - Le Consulat

Dine at the charming Le Consulat, a picturesque café with vintage flair and a storybook facade that’s been captured in countless romantic paintings. This iconic Montmartre spot is ideal for a casual lunch while people-watching from its terrace.

  • Recommended Duration: 1–1.5 hours
  • Travel Time to next stop: 10 mins walk to Moulin Rouge/rue Lepic

🏛 Afternoon - Moulin Rouge photo stop + wander rue Lepic

Snap a photo at the famous Moulin Rouge, then meander down Rue Lepic, a charming street filled with hidden boutiques and cafés once frequented by Van Gogh. It’s a romantic way to enjoy Montmartre beyond the tourist trail.

  • Recommended Duration: 1 hour
  • Travel Time to next stop: 25 mins by Metro to Girafe Restaurant

🌇 Evening - Dinner at Girafe (Eiffel Tower view)

Treat yourselves to an unforgettable dinner at Girafe, a sleek art deco restaurant with a panoramic view of the Eiffel Tower. As the sun sets and the lights begin to twinkle, this elegant setting becomes one of the most romantic dining experiences in Paris.

  • Recommended Duration: 1.5–2 hours
  • Travel Time to next stop: 10 mins walk to Pont Alexandre III

🌙 Night - Seine walk under the stars near Pont Alexandre III

End the night with a moonlit stroll along the Seine near Pont Alexandre III, Paris’s most ornate bridge. The golden statues, softly glowing street lamps, and reflections on the river create an enchanting ambiance for couples.

  • Recommended Duration: 45 mins–1 hour
  • End of Day

🗓 Day 2

☕️ Breakfast - Café Kitsuné in Palais Royal garden

Start your day with specialty coffee at Café Kitsuné, tucked into the elegant arcades of Palais Royal. Sip your espresso in the garden among quiet fountains and symmetrical tree-lined paths — a peaceful and stylish Parisian moment.

  • Recommended Duration: 45 minutes
  • Travel Time to next stop: 10 mins walk to Sainte-Chapelle

🌞 Morning - Sainte-Chapelle

Sainte-Chapelle is a Gothic jewel, with walls of kaleidoscopic stained glass that dazzle in the morning sun. It's smaller and more intimate than other churches, perfect for a quiet, awe-inspiring experience that feels like stepping into a jewel box.

  • Recommended Duration: 45 mins–1 hour
  • Travel Time to next stop: 15 mins walk or 5 mins Metro to Ralph’s

🍽 Lunch - Ralph’s (in Ralph Lauren mansion)

Dine in the romantic courtyard of Ralph’s, set in a restored mansion on Boulevard Saint-Germain. With American-French fusion cuisine and a chic, garden-like setting, this hidden spot is a surprising escape from the city’s bustle.

  • Recommended Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Travel Time to next stop: 10 mins walk to Pont des Arts

🏛 Afternoon - Pont des Arts + Tuileries Gardens

Cross the Pont des Arts, once known for its love locks, and stroll into the Tuileries Gardens for a romantic walk among fountains, sculptures, and flowerbeds. This is the perfect midday pause with open skies and classic Parisian views.

  • Recommended Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Travel Time to next stop: 15 mins walk to Seine Cruise dock

🌇 Evening - Romantic dinner cruise on the Seine

Float past Paris’s most iconic sights while enjoying a candlelit dinner aboard a bateau-mouche. As the sun sets and monuments light up, you’ll have front-row seats to the city’s evening magic — a timeless way to celebrate romance in Paris.

  • Recommended Duration: 2 hours
  • Travel Time to next stop: 10–15 mins to Saint-Germain

🌙 Night - Jazz bar in Saint-Germain-des-Prés

Cap off the night with live jazz in one of Saint-Germain-des-Prés’ intimate clubs like Le Caveau de la Huchette or Chez Papa Jazz. These spots offer candlelit tables, vintage ambiance, and sultry rhythms — the perfect end to your romantic Paris escape.

  • Recommended Duration: 1.5–2 hours
  • End of Day
